Sports Page

Sports have been a big part of my life since I was a kid. I have played many different sports, but the ones that I have played the most are soccer, basketball and track.

My Soccer Journey

I have been playing soccer since I can remember because my dad is a coach. I didn't start playing organized soccer until I was about 10. From that point on, I played for clubs such as GPS, Valeo FC, and NEFC. I have also tried out for teams such as Boston Bolts and New England Revolution, but never fully played for either.

I also played highschool soccer for every year except my senior year.

My goal tallies for each season are as followed:

  • Freshman Year: 5 goals + 3 assists
  • Sophomore Year: 12 goals + 12 assists
  • Junior Year: 25 goals + 15 assists

I now play division 1 soccer here at Stonehill and have been starting since my Freshman year.
